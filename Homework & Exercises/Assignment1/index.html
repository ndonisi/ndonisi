B.L.A.T.

A sammy to cure what ails ya

Make a B.L.A.T. for lunch because you are definitely too hangry for your next zoom class.

Time until you eat: ~12 mins

You need bread, mayo & the following:

1.BACON

-Bake on foil in the oven for 10 mins, procrastinate on your phone while its cooking, toss foil, rinse tray. Done!

2.LETTUCE

-Look! A vegetable! Bonus points!

3.AVOCADO

-Tasty-tasty, fatty-fatty. Smash on the bread and add salt n pepper. You dont even need mayo if you have a lot of 'cado.

4.TOMATO

-If you do not like tomatoes, please navigate away from this page with the other heathens
